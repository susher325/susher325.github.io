Biography

Hello! My name is Sam Usher and I am a senior at Tufts University studying Human Factors Engineering. I am passionate about creating safer and more enjoyable user experiences through user-centered design. Through my computer science and Human Factors classes at Tufts, I have learned a variety of skills including coding, 3D modeling, how to conduct usability testing, prototyping, designing user interfaces, and now, web design!

As someone with many different interests, I have taken advantadge of the oppurtunity to work in a variety of different fields such as UX Research, Neuroscience Market Research, and Human Factors medical device research. This semester, I am interning at UL-Wiklund, a Human Factors company that mainly works on usability testing for medical devices, where I will assist with setting up for and conducting focus groups, interviews, usability tests, and writing reports. Prior to this, I work at Nielsen Consumer Neuroscience, a neuromarketing company that conducted neuroscience market research for Fortune 500 companies.

In my free time, I enjoy skiing, reading, spending time with my friends and family, and exploring new places in Boston.
